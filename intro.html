Understanding Color Variants

Hue

Hue refers to the pure pigment of a color, without tint or shade.

Shade

Shade refers to how much black is added into the hue, darkening the color.

Tint

Tint refers to how much white is added to a color, lightening it.

Tone

Tone is the result of adding both black and white (gray) to a hue.

Color Temperature

Warm colors contain yellow and red; cool colors have blue or green tones.
